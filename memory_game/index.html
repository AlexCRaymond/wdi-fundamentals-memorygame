Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

About

Although this is a pretty old game, it has stood the test of time and will still rock your socks off! Okay maybe not but it's still a really fun game to play with your friends. And hey, make it more interesting by betting them they can't match three in a row or THEY have to buy the pizza you just ordered. Or the beer. Whatever you want it's YOUR GAME! Have fun!
